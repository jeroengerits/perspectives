# Benefits

Use this skill to identify why an option might work and where it creates value.

## When To Use

Use this skill for:

- identifying the strongest benefits of an option
- explaining where value appears in practice
- testing whether an idea has strategic upside
- clarifying the conditions under which success is realistic

## When NOT To Use

Do not use this skill for:

- hand-waving away known risks
- making unsupported claims of success
- supplying raw facts instead of interpreted upside
- issuing the final decision on behalf of `facilitator`

## Purpose

Benefits ensures that promising options get a fair reading instead of being dismissed too early.

## Trigger Signals

Use this skill when the task needs:

- the strongest case for why an option could work
- concrete sources of value, leverage, or strategic fit
- conditions that make success realistic
- upside analysis without collapsing into generic optimism

## Collaboration Contract

When used inside `perspective`:

- this skill is a required feedback input
- focus on practical upside
- connect each benefit to the stated objective
- do not argue away known risks

When used alone:

- explain value clearly without claiming certainty

## Input Contract

Expect a brief that includes:

- the option, plan, or decision under review
- the objective it is supposed to serve
- relevant constraints or tradeoffs
- any known facts, assumptions, or open questions

## Required Feedback

When called by `perspective`, return:

- `Primary Benefits`
- `Value Creation`
- `Conditions For Success`
- `Positive Spillovers`

## Focus

Prioritize:

- benefits
- upside
- strategic fit
- efficiency gains
- user value
- leverage
- positive second-order effects

## Rules

- Be concrete rather than cheerleading.
- Explain how the benefit appears in practice.
- Distinguish real upside from wishful thinking.
- Tie each benefit to the user's stated objective or decision.

## Do Not

- ignore constraints
- make vague claims like "this is great"
- argue away known risks
- turn absence of evidence into proof of upside

## Output Format

- `Primary Benefits`
- `Value Creation`
- `Conditions For Success`
- `Positive Spillovers`
